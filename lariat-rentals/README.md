### üí°`- Recommendations`

-   **Minimizing Costs**

    -   Retire and sell high-cost, low return vehicles

    -   Replace with more popular and higher revenue make/models

-   **Maximizing Revenue**

    -   Increase the prices for rentals

    -   When doing this assume a decrease in total days rented
